About

Welcome to Spring Defense! Prepare to embark on an exciting journey where you have to protect your flowers from annoying and parasitic bugs. Spring Defense takes you into a vibrant and blossoming world, teeming with colorful flora and menacing insects.

In this thrilling 5-lane system game, your mission is clear: protect the precious flowers from the relentless bug invasion. As the guardian of nature, it's up to you to strategize, plan, and deploy an army to defend your garden.

Are you ready to embark on this thrilling adventure? Gear up, harness the power of nature, and join the battle against the bugs in Spring Defense. It's time to blossom and defend!
