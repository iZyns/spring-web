About

Welcome to Spring Defense! Prepare to embark on an exciting journey where you have to protect your flowers from annoying and parasitic bugs. Spring Defense takes you into a vibrant and blossoming world, teeming with colorful flora and menacing insects.

In this thrilling 5-lane system game, your mission is clear: protect the precious flowers from the relentless bug invasion. As the guardian of nature, it's up to you to strategize, plan, and deploy an army to defend your garden.

Are you ready to embark on this thrilling adventure? Gear up, harness the power of nature, and join the battle against the bugs in Spring Defense. It's time to blossom and defend!

Development Log

  • April 17,2023:
    Brainstormed ideas for the project and came up with game mechanics for the conceptual prototype.

  • April 24, 2023:
    Finished the first digital prototype of the game.
    Implemented 4 main game mechanics: drag and drop UI, enemy spawning, static currency system, and shooting mechanic.
    All prototypes were kept in separate builds.

  • May 1, 2023:
    Completed the second digital prototype.
    Further developed the 4 main game mechanics.
    Added more enemy types to the game.
    Continued keeping prototypes in separate builds.

  • May 6, 2023:
    Combined the working prototypes into a single build, creating a working game in the alpha stage.
    Incorporated images and textures into the game.

  • May 8, 2023:
    Conducted an alpha playtest and received feedback from the players.
    Made necessary changes to the game based on the feedback.

  • May 13, 2023:
    Improved the game based on the initial playtest feedback.
    Added different enemy types, shooting mechanics, and a main menu to the game.

  • May 15, 2023:
    Conducted a beta playtest and gathered more feedback from the players.

  • May 22, 2023:
    Added multiple characters to the game, each with unique abilities.
    Implemented different types of projectiles that deal varying amounts of damage.
    Introduced multiple enemy spawns with different intervals and health.
    Updated the user interface (UI) to enhance the player experience.

  • May 30, 2023:
    Implemented a feature where enemies drop coins that players must collect to earn more points.
    Addressed any remaining bugs related to the game and the UI.
    Enhanced the UI of the main menu.
    Added sound effects and background music to enhance the overall game experience.
    Applied final touch-ups to the game.
